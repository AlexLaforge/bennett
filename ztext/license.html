Ztext Licensing

Ztext has two licenses: Open Source license and the Commercial Developer license.

Open Source license

The Open Source license is designed for you to use Ztext to build open source and personal projects. It is free and requires no purchase. The Ztext Open Source license is GPLv3. The GPLv3 has many terms, but the most important is how it is sticky when you distribute your work publicly.

From the GPL Frequently Asked Questions

If you release the modified version to the public in some way, the GPL requires you to make the modified source code available to the program's users, under the GPL.

Releasing your project that uses Ztext under the GPLv3, in turn, requires your project to be licensed under the GPLv3. If you are okay with this, feel free to use Ztext under the GPLv3, without purchasing a Commercial Developer license.

Commercial Developer license

The Ztext Commercial Developer license is designed for you to use Ztext in commercial products and applications, without the provisions of the GPLv3. With the Commercial Developer license, your code is kept proprietary, to yourself. If you want to use Ztext to develop commercial sites, themes, projects, and applications, the commercial license is the appropriate license.

The Commercial Developer license is a one-time payment of $10.00 for one developer. The Ztext Commercial Developer license is for individual developers. Each individual developer or team member needs to purchase a separate license.

Read the Ztext Commercial License Agreement below for complete terms and conditions.

Purchasing a commercial license

Purchasing takes a minute. Our purchasing form accepts credit cards or PayPal. Once purchased, you’ll receive a Commercial Developer License PDF and you will be all set to use Ztext in your commercial applications. With the purchase of a commercial license:

  • You may use Ztext in as many commercial applications you like.
  • You may use Ztext in your own commercial applications and products. For example: premium WordPress other CMS themes, plugins and templates.
  • Customers and users of your products do not need to purchase their own license, so long as they are not developing their own commercial products with Ztext.
  • Commercial Developer Licenses are priced per team member. A team member is someone on your team working on a project that uses Ztext.
